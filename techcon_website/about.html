Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology professionals and enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest and most respected conferences in the tech industry, attracting thousands of participants from around the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire innovation, connect minds, and drive the future of technology through inclusive, cutting-edge conversations and demonstrations.

Past Speakers

Here are some of the thought leaders who have graced the TechCon stage:

Dr. Ada Lin – Renowned AI researcher and developer of neural translation systems. She spoke on ethical AI development at TechCon 2022.

Carlos Ramirez – Lead architect at CloudWorks, who shared insights on building scalable infrastructure in 2021.

Rina Patel – Cybersecurity expert and advisor to Fortune 500 companies, known for her 2020 keynote on digital privacy.
